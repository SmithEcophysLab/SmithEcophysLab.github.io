Photosynthesis and respiration by plants are major drivers of the global carbon cycle.

We study how expected future climates will influence plants' abilities to uptake carbon dioxide and, in turn, how this will alter expectations of future climate.

Acclimation

Plant responses to environmental change may differ over time as a result of acclimation. We study how plant responses to temperature, light, carbon dioxide, and fertilization changes over time of exposure. We have found that consideration of acclimation can have a large impact on future projections.
