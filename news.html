February 13, 2024: A lab rebranding

After months of discussion and debate, the lab has settled on a rebranding. We are now the Physiology for Understanding the Functioning of Ecosystems at Texas Tech University Lab! The focus of the lab remains unchanged, but the name and logo (see below) are now way more fun! We can't wait to continue incorporating plant physiology, ecosystem ecology, and fancy cakes!
